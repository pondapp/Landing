Welcome to Pond

Make Real Connections!

Even with so much online connectivity and social media at our fingertips, approaching new people and making real world connections is harder than it should be. Pond solves that problem by pairing you up with someone also looking to meet. Just hop on and tell us when you want to meet someone, let us take care of the rest!

No Time Wasted.

Though there are numerous social meet up apps, people get caught up with endless swiping and indecision over where and when to meet. Pond gives you a match and choses a location for you. Just say when!

Anywhere. Anytime.

Pond not only runs on mobile devices, but also through your browser! Want to take a break from a study sesh and meet someone in the library? Just sign on and get a match! Want to meet someone in between classes for coffee? Just sign on and get a match!
